Celebrating Coffee
Since 1987

The Weekend Coffee Club has been a vibrant celebration of coffee culture, bringing together industry professionals, enthusiasts, and innovators from around the world. Over the decades, it has served as a hub for sharing knowledge, showcasing cutting-edge technology, and exploring the latest trends in coffee production, roasting, and brewing.

From insightful keynote speeches to interactive workshops and tasting sessions, the event has fostered a deep appreciation for the art and science of coffee. Whether highlighting sustainability efforts, honoring skilled baristas, or unveiling groundbreaking advancements, the conference continues to uphold its rich legacy of championing coffee excellence and community engagement.
